A spider-man themed ao3 site skin :)

How to set a site skin:

1. Sign into ao3 if you haven't, click on your name at the top of the page and go to "Dashboard"
2. Tap on "Skins" and then click on "Create site skin"
3. My site skins use both Wizard and CSS, so first go to the "Use Wizard" section.
4. On the "about" section you'll need to type a unique site skin name. If your name isn't unique, you'll get a error message when you try to save your site skin. I usually just put the name of the skin and then my username at the end.
5. Scroll until you find the "Colors" field, then copy and paste the color codes. After that, scroll to the end of the page and click on the "Submit" button. This will save your site skin.
6. Click on the "Edit" button at the bottom of the page and scroll to the "CSS" labelled field, then copy and paste the CSS code. Scroll to the end of the page and click on "Submit" again to save the changes.
7. At the bottom of the page, click on the "Use" button.

That's it! You now will have that skin on whenever you use ao3 in any device.
